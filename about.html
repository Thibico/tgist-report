ABOUT THE ASIA FOUNDATION’S URBAN SAFETY PROJECT

The Asia Foundation’s Urban Safety Project aims to support Myanmar's nascent transition to democracy by strengthening the effectiveness and responsiveness of government actors at the township level in addressing people’s urban safety needs. The project does so by bringing together multiple stakeholders critical to urban safety – government officials, parliamentarians, municipal authorities, the General Administration Department, the police, and the public – to reshape local responses to the specific safety concerns impacting the population.

ABOUT THIBI
